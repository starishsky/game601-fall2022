Hypertext Fiction: The Bakery Collaboration
You're the daughter of a well-renowned bakery, Orsachiotto. When a local broadcasting company asks you to cater for an event, you jump on the opportunity. One catch: you have to work with one of the rival bakeries in order to accomodate for all of their employees. Choose between one of the bakeries, Agnellini Dolce or Pasticceria Lupo, and maybe don't tell your dad about it (because of the rivalry).
